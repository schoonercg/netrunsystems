Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Compliance Reporter compare to native Azure compliance tools?

While Azure provides basic compliance tools within a single tenant, the Netrun Systems Compliance Reporter offers cross-tenant assessment, multi-framework support, automated evidence collection, and consultant-specific features like white-labeled reporting. Our solution is specifically designed for consultants and MSPs managing compliance across multiple client environments.

Can I customize compliance frameworks for specific client requirements?

Yes, the Enterprise plan includes support for custom compliance frameworks. You can create custom frameworks based on specific client requirements or industry standards not included in our standard library. Our compliance specialists can assist with framework development and implementation.

How does evidence collection work?

The Compliance Reporter automatically collects evidence through Azure APIs, capturing configurations, policies, logs, and other artifacts required to demonstrate compliance. Evidence is organized by control and framework, with timestamps and source information for audit traceability. For controls requiring manual evidence, the system provides structured workflows for collection and documentation.

Can I use the Compliance Reporter for client audits?

Absolutely. The Compliance Reporter is designed to support audit processes with comprehensive, audit-ready reports that can be shared directly with auditors. Reports include detailed control assessments, evidence documentation, remediation status, and historical compliance trends. Many clients report significant reductions in audit preparation time and improved audit outcomes.

How frequently are compliance assessments updated?

Compliance assessments are updated continuously based on changes in the environment and control status. The system performs daily scans for configuration changes that might impact compliance, with real-time updates to compliance scores and dashboards. Full assessments are conducted weekly, with comprehensive reports generated automatically or on-demand.
