Frequently Asked Questions

What is the NIST Cybersecurity Framework?

The NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF) is a voluntary framework consisting of standards, guidelines, and best practices to manage cybersecurity risk. It was developed by the National Institute of Standards and Technology and focuses on five core functions: Identify, Protect, Detect, Respond, and Recover.

Do I need a ChatGPT subscription to use the assistant?

Yes, you'll need a ChatGPT Plus subscription to access our custom GPT. This ensures you have the most advanced AI capabilities and reliable access to the assistant.

Is my data secure when using the assistant?

We take data security seriously. The assistant operates within OpenAI's security framework, and we do not store your conversations. For details on how your data is handled, please refer to our Privacy Policy and OpenAI's data handling practices.

How is the assistant kept up to date?

Our team of cybersecurity experts regularly updates the assistant with the latest NIST CSF guidance, best practices, and implementation strategies to ensure you always have access to current information.

Can the assistant help with other security frameworks?

While the assistant specializes in the NIST Cybersecurity Framework, it can provide general guidance on how NIST CSF maps to other frameworks like ISO 27001, CIS Controls, and CMMC. For detailed assistance with other frameworks, please contact us about our consulting services.
