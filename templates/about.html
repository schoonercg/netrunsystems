About Netrun Systems

From a valley famed for its laid-back mystique, proving that automation, ethics, and art can belong on the same team.

Our Mission

At Netrun Systems, we're showing that high-impact tech doesn't have to be born in Silicon Valley. It can come from a place as eclectic as Ojai — for real humans who need real-world solutions. We specialize in automation, cloud governance, and innovative solutions that solve real-world problems while keeping people at the center.

What We Do

Interkon

A cross-tenant cloud governance platform for MSPs, delivering enterprise-grade security and cost optimization—no matter how many client tenants they juggle.

Interfyx

A small-business optimization dashboard that integrates with Square, QuickBooks, and other common tools, translating data into insights people actually understand.

Interkast

A social-automation and generative-media studio that keeps marketing campaigns genuinely social rather than "spammy."

Our Story

Founded with the vision of bridging the gap between complex technology and practical business needs, Netrun Systems emerged from the recognition that many organizations struggle with cloud governance and security compliance. Our team combines deep technical expertise with a genuine understanding of business challenges to deliver solutions that actually work.

Located in the beautiful Ojai Valley of California, we draw inspiration from our surroundings to create technology solutions that are both powerful and harmonious with human needs. We believe that the best technology solutions are those that seamlessly integrate into people's workflows and enhance their capabilities rather than creating additional complexity.
