Frequently Asked Questions

How is Nexus different from Azure Lighthouse?

Nexus enhances Azure Lighthouse with proprietary security controls, a unified management dashboard, just-in-time privileged access, comprehensive audit logging, and specialized add-ons for cost optimization and compliance. While Lighthouse provides basic cross-tenant delegation, Nexus delivers a complete governance solution.

What's the implementation timeline?

Most consultancies complete implementation within 4-6 weeks, with core platform deployment taking just 1 week. You'll see immediate efficiency gains from day one, with full ROI typically realized within 6 months.

Do my clients need to install anything?

No. Nexus requires no agent installation on managed resources. Implementation uses Azure Lighthouse delegation mechanisms with enhanced security controls, making the onboarding process seamless for your clients.

How does the pricing work for my clients?

You have flexibility in how you present Nexus to your clients. You can resell Nexus tenant subscriptions directly, include them in your managed services pricing, or have clients subscribe directly through the Azure Marketplace while specifying your consultant tenant.

Is Nexus secure? How does it protect sensitive client data?

Security is our foundation. Nexus enhances Azure Lighthouse's security with just-in-time access, approval workflows, comprehensive audit logging, and anomaly detection. Our architecture follows zero-trust principles and maintains strict tenant isolation. We never store client credentials or sensitive data.
