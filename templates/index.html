About Netrun Systems

Built by Consultants, for Consultants

Founded by Daniel Garza, a former Microsoft Senior Technology Consultant and digital systems architect at Rackspace Technology, Netrun Systems is revolutionizing cross-tenant governance in Microsoft Azure environments.

Our flagship product, Netrun Systems Nexus, is a comprehensive Azure Marketplace solution that enables secure, efficient management across multiple client environments. Built on Azure Lighthouse but enhanced with proprietary security controls and management capabilities, Nexus provides a unified dashboard, enhanced security, and specialized add-ons for cost optimization and compliance.

We've experienced the challenges of multi-tenant management firsthand and built the solution we always wished existed.
