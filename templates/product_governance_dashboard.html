Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Governance Dashboard compare to Azure Policy and Management Groups?

While Azure Policy and Management Groups provide governance capabilities within a single tenant, the Netrun Systems Governance Dashboard delivers cross-tenant governance with unified policy management, standardized controls, and comprehensive reporting across all your client environments. Our solution is specifically designed for consultants and MSPs who need to implement consistent governance practices at scale.

Can I customize governance policies for different clients?

Yes, the Governance Dashboard supports policy inheritance with exception handling, allowing you to implement standardized baseline policies across all clients while accommodating specific requirements for individual environments. You can create policy hierarchies with inheritance rules and manage exceptions through a centralized interface.

How does policy enforcement work?

The Governance Dashboard provides multiple enforcement options for policies, including audit-only (monitoring without enforcement), soft enforcement (allowing with notification), and hard enforcement (preventing non-compliant actions). You can configure enforcement levels for each policy and client environment based on your governance requirements.

Can I white-label the Governance Dashboard for my clients?

Yes, the Professional and Enterprise plans include white-labeling capabilities. You can customize dashboards and reports with your branding, allowing you to present governance as part of your managed service offering. The Enterprise plan also supports custom dashboard development for specific client requirements.

How does the implementation process work?

Implementation begins with an assessment of your current governance practices and requirements. Our team works with you to develop standardized policies and configure the Governance Dashboard for your specific needs. We provide templates and best practices to accelerate implementation, with most clients fully operational within 2-4 weeks.
