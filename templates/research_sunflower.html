Sunflower: Garden & Landscape Management Device

A solar-powered IoT device for real-time environmental monitoring in gardens, farms, and greenhouses.

Overview

Sunflower is a modular, solar-powered device based on the ESP32 microcontroller. It collects temperature, humidity, soil moisture, and sunlight data, transmitting it to the cloud or a master node for analytics and visualization.

Key Features

  • ESP32-based with Wi-Fi and Bluetooth
  • Solar-powered with battery backup
  • Environmental sensors: DHT-11, soil moisture, photoresistors
  • Peer-to-peer mesh networking (ESP-NOW)
  • Azure IoT Hub integration for cloud analytics
  • Weather-resistant, modular enclosure
  • Low-power operation and OTA updates

Architecture

  • Data collection via sensors
  • Local processing and power management
  • Wi-Fi/ESP-NOW for data transmission
  • Cloud storage and dashboarding (Azure SQL/Cosmos DB, Power BI)

Use Cases

  • Smart gardens and greenhouses
  • Precision agriculture and farm monitoring
  • Environmental research and education
  • Automated irrigation and climate control
