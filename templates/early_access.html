Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the Early Access Program last?

The Early Access Program is expected to run for 3-4 months before general availability. Early access participants will receive exclusive benefits that continue beyond the program period, including guaranteed pricing, priority support, and ongoing input into our product roadmap.

Is there a cost to participate in the Early Access Program?

Yes, Early Access participants pay a reduced rate compared to our general availability pricing. This early adopter pricing is locked in for 12 months after the program ends, providing significant long-term savings. The exact pricing will be discussed during the selection process.

What are the technical requirements for participation?

Participants should be actively managing multiple Azure tenants and have experience with Azure Lighthouse or cross-tenant management. Our solutions are designed to enhance existing Azure environments without requiring significant changes to your infrastructure.

How much time commitment is required?

We recommend allocating 4-6 hours for initial onboarding and implementation, followed by 1-2 hours per month for feedback sessions and product updates. The actual time savings from using our solutions typically far exceeds this investment within the first month.

Can I invite my clients to participate?

Yes, we encourage you to involve select clients in the Early Access Program. This provides valuable feedback from both the consultant and end-customer perspectives. We can work with you to create appropriate messaging and documentation for your clients.
