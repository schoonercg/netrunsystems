Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 30, 2025

1. Introduction

Netrun Systems ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our services, including our cross-tenant governance solutions and website.

  • Payment information for service subscriptions

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Providing and maintaining our services

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Employee training on data protection

We may share your information with:

  • Third parties with your explicit consent

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your data

We may transfer your information to servers located outside your country. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international data transfers.

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
