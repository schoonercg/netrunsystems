Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Cost Optimizer compare to Azure Cost Management?

While Azure Cost Management provides basic cost visibility within a single tenant, the Netrun Systems Cost Optimizer delivers cross-tenant analysis, AI-driven recommendations, and consultant-specific features like white-labeled reporting and client-specific optimization strategies. Our solution is specifically designed for consultants and MSPs managing multiple client environments.

How quickly will I see results?

Most customers identify significant savings opportunities within the first 24-48 hours of implementation. The initial assessment typically identifies "quick win" opportunities that can be implemented immediately, with more strategic recommendations developing as the system collects more usage data over time.

Can I white-label the Cost Optimizer for my clients?

Yes, the Professional and Enterprise plans include white-labeling capabilities. You can customize reports and dashboards with your branding, allowing you to present cost optimization as part of your managed service offering.

Does the Cost Optimizer require any agents or on-premises components?

No, the Cost Optimizer is entirely cloud-based and requires no agents or on-premises components. It leverages Azure's native APIs and the Nexus platform's cross-tenant capabilities to collect and analyze cost data.

How does the implementation process work?

Implementation is seamless through the Nexus platform. Once enabled, the Cost Optimizer begins analyzing your managed tenants immediately. The system provides both manual and automated implementation options for recommendations, allowing you to review and approve changes before they're applied.
