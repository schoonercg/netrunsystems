Podcast Co-Host (AI)

An AI-powered assistant for podcast planning, guest research, live transcription, and content summarization.

Overview

The Podcast Co-Host leverages advanced language models to assist podcast creators with show planning, guest research, real-time transcription, and post-production content summarization. It integrates with popular podcast platforms and provides actionable insights for hosts and producers.

Key Features

  • Automated show planning and topic suggestion
  • Guest research and background briefings
  • Live transcription and timestamped notes
  • Content summarization and highlight extraction
  • Integration with podcast publishing platforms
  • Speaker identification and analytics

Architecture

  • Speech-to-text engine (Whisper, Azure Speech)
  • LLM-powered content analysis and summarization
  • Web dashboard for hosts and producers
  • APIs for platform integration

Use Cases

  • Podcast creators and producers
  • Media companies and content studios
  • Live event coverage and transcription
  • Accessible podcasting for hearing-impaired audiences
